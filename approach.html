Establish Project Goals

I take care in listening to my clients early and often in order to establish goals that are realistic and deliver outstanding projects. It is important to reflect back on these goals throughout the entire process.

Define & Research Users

I pride myself on user centered design practices. Before I even begin to visualize what your product will look like, I conduct a thorough user persona report that outlines the strengths and weaknesses of your current and future users.

Wireframe

Wireframes are a critical tool in the design process. This is the first time we commit ideas to paper and begin to layout the interface and interactions. I begin with a very broad approach, then dial in the details.

Iterate

An iterative design process is crucial to the success of any project. Quickly iterating ideas and concepts allow us to validate our designs early and identify any problem areas before we move into development.

Clickable Wireframe

I strive to stay on the forefront of best design practices. A clickable wireframe is a cutting-edge tool that allows us to create interactive prototypes without writing a single line of code. Interactive wireframes can even be downloaded as a mobile application.

User Testing

User testing allows us to validate design decisions, make necessary adjustments, and guage user satisfaction throuh a series of exercises. The best feedback during a project often comes from user testing.

Iterate

User testing often identifies weaknesses in a design. This is why I test early and iterate quickly before it's too late. I have gone through fifteen versions of wireframes before in order to ensure a great user experience.

Visual Design

Once the layout and interactive patterns are established, I begin a series of exercises to explore the visual identity of a project. You will receive pixel perfect designs that will be sure to impress your audience.

Front-End Development

My process concludes with developing an HTML prototype for your team. I use modern development technologies such as Bootstrap, SASS, and jQuery to develop my projects.
