Building Energy Benchmarking

The Philadelphia Mayors Office of Sustainability released a building energy dataset for 1,880 properties across the city. The data consists of factors such as energy intake, emissions, and energy star ratings. The Building Energy Benchmarking application is an interactive online tool that visualizes the data with interactive charts and maps. Users can get a full energy report card for specific buildings, or compare data across multiple buildings.
